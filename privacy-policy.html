Privacy Policy

Seedemo · Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Seedemo (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Seedemo mobile application for iOS (“App”). By using the App, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Summary. Seedemo is built for capturing music ideas (voice and text). We use your account to sync content you choose to store with our service providers. We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your information for third‑party advertising or “tracking” as described in Apple’s App Privacy labels.

1. Who we are

Seedemo is operated by an independent developer. For privacy requests, contact us at: jxmikewang@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

We collect the following categories of information, consistent with disclosures in the App Store:

2.1 Account and contact information

  • Name. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we may receive a display name associated with your account.
  • Email address. Used to create and manage your account (for example, via Sign in with Apple or Google).
  • User ID. A unique identifier assigned to your account in our backend to distinguish your data from other users.

2.2 Content you create (“User Content”)

  • Audio data. Voice recordings and related audio you capture in the App, stored locally on your device and, when you use cloud features, backed up to our infrastructure.
  • Other user content. Text notes, lyrics, tags, transcripts generated from your recordings, version history of your seeds, and similar materials you save in the App.

2.3 Purchases and subscriptions

If you purchase Seedemo Pro or other paid offerings, Apple processes payments through In‑App Purchase. We do not receive your full payment card number. We and our subscription partner receive information about whether your subscription is active and related purchase history as needed to unlock features in the App.

2.4 Information we do not intend to collect

The App is not designed to collect contacts from your address book, precise location, health data, or photos from your library for its core features. If we add such features later, we will update this policy and, where required, request permission in the App.

3. How we use information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • App functionality: authenticate you; sync and store your seeds; provide playback, search, tags, trash/recovery windows, and subscription entitlements; operate security and abuse prevention; provide customer support when you contact us.
  • AI features: when you request transcription or other AI-assisted features, audio or text you submit may be sent to model providers solely to return results to you, subject to fair-use and plan limits described in the App.

We do not use your information for third‑party advertising, and we do not sell your personal information. We do not use User IDs or other data for “tracking” as defined in Apple’s App Tracking Transparency framework.

4. Service providers (processors)

We rely on vendors that process data on our behalf under contractual safeguards:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and cloud storage for your account and content.
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple and In‑App Purchase.
  • Google — Google sign‑in, if you choose it.
  • OpenAI — speech‑to‑text (for example, Whisper) when you request transcription of audio.
  • Anthropic — text analysis for certain Pro features (for example, semantic search or insights), using text derived from your content, routed through our servers.
  • RevenueCat — subscription status and purchase validation to manage Pro access.

Each provider’s use of data is governed by their own policies and our agreements with them. We only send what is needed to provide the feature you use.

5. Legal bases (EEA / UK users)

If applicable law requires a “legal basis,” we process personal data to perform our contract with you (provide the App), based on our legitimate interests in operating and securing the service (where not overridden by your rights), or with your consent where required (for example, optional communications if we introduce them).

6. Retention

We keep your information as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the App. Content you delete may be retained in trash for a limited period (for example, up to 30 days) before permanent deletion, as described in the App. Backups may persist for a short additional period in ordinary course. If you ask us to delete your account, we will delete or anonymize personal data unless we must retain certain records for legal reasons.

7. Security

We use technical and organizational measures appropriate to the nature of the service (including encryption in transit and access controls). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal information, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, email us at the address in Section 1. You may also adjust permissions in iOS Settings for the App where applicable.

California residents: California law may grant you additional rights (including “Do Not Sell” — we do not sell personal information). You may designate an authorized agent where permitted by law.

9. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International transfers

We and our service providers may process information in the United States and other countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for cross‑border transfers.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the new date at the top and, where appropriate, provide notice in the App. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
